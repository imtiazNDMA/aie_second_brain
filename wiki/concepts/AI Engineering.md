# AI Engineering

## Definition

Applied discipline focused on building, deploying, and operating AI systems after the model itself “works.” Blends software engineering, data engineering, and product practices to make ML models reliable in production.

## Pillars

- **Data operations** — Collection, labeling, cleaning, augmentation, and governance feeding continuous improvement loops.
- **Training/Adaptation** — [[Distributed Training]], [[Fine-Tuning]], and evaluation workflows that map offline metrics to business KPIs.
- **Deployment & Ops** — Selecting batch vs [[Real-Time Deployment]], applying [[Inference Optimization]], and enforcing observability.
- **Monitoring & [[Continuous Training]]** — Detect drift, trigger retraining, and coordinate incident response.
