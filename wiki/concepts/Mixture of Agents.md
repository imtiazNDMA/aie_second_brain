# Mixture of Agents

## Definition

Architecture where multiple specialized agents (or fine-tuned experts) collaborate under a shared router/controller, similar to Mixture of Experts but at the workflow level.

## Highlights

- Lets teams fine-tune lightweight specialists (summarization, coding, safety) and combine outputs via arbitration rules.
- Requires governance and monitoring to keep agent coordination safe.
