# Gaussian Mixture Model

## Definition

Probabilistic model expressing data as a weighted sum of Gaussian components, typically trained via Expectation-Maximization.

## Highlights

- Captures multi-modal structure better than single Gaussian assumptions.
- Foundational for density estimation, clustering, and anomaly detection.
