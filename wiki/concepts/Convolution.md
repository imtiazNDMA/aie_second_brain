# Convolution

## Definition

Operation that applies sliding kernels over structured inputs (for example images or sequences) to extract local patterns with shared weights.

## Why It Matters

- Encodes translation-aware feature extraction with far fewer parameters than dense layers.
- Forms the core building block of CNNs used for vision and time-series tasks.
