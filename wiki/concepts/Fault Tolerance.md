# Fault Tolerance

## Definition

Ability of a system to continue serving acceptable functionality when one or more components fail.

## Why It Matters

- Converts partial failures into degraded-but-operational behavior instead of full outages.
- Requires redundancy, failover policies, and recovery automation.
