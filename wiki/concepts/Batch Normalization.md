# Batch Normalization

## Definition

Normalization layer that standardizes intermediate activations per mini-batch, then applies learnable scale and shift parameters.

## Why It Matters

- Stabilizes optimization and supports deeper architectures.
- Can reduce sensitivity to initialization and learning-rate settings.
