# ROUGE

## Definition

Recall-Oriented Understudy for Gisting Evaluation (ROUGE) is a family of overlap metrics that scores generated text against reference text, commonly used in summarization and RAG output checks.

## Why It Matters

- Captures coverage of reference content in generated outputs.
- Complements precision-oriented metrics like [[BLEU]] when evaluating answer completeness.
