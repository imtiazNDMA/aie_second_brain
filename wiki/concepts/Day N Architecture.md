# Day N Architecture

## Definition

Mature architecture stage where systems are restructured for sustained scale, resilience, cost control, and operational automation.

## Why It Matters

- Captures growth-driven refactors that MVP designs cannot absorb safely.
- Makes reliability and operability explicit as traffic and team size increase.
