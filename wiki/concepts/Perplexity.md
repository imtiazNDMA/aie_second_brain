# Perplexity

## Definition

Intrinsic language-model metric derived from token likelihood; lower perplexity indicates the model assigns higher probability to observed text.

## Why It Matters

- Useful for comparing base/fine-tuned model fit on held-out corpora.
- Works best with task-specific metrics and human evaluation for end-user quality.
