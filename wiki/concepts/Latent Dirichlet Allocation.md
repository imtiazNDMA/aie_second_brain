# Latent Dirichlet Allocation

## Definition

Generative probabilistic model that represents documents as mixtures of latent topics, each topic being a distribution over words.

## Notes

- Uses variational Bayes or collapsed Gibbs sampling for inference.
- Featured in the book’s unsupervised learning chapters as a bridge between density estimation and interpretable topics.
