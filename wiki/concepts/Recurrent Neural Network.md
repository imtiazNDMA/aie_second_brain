# Recurrent Neural Network

## Definition

Neural architecture that processes sequence elements step-by-step while carrying a hidden state across timesteps.

## Why It Matters

- Captures temporal dependencies in sequential data.
- Establishes the foundation for GRU/LSTM and encoder-decoder sequence models.
