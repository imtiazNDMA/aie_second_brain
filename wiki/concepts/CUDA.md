# CUDA

## Definition

NVIDIA parallel-computing platform used by frameworks like [[PyTorch]] to accelerate tensor operations on GPUs.

## Why It Matters

- Enables much faster training and inference for large models.
- Requires explicit device management and tensor placement in training code.
