# Confusion Matrix

## Definition

Tabular summary of predicted versus true classes that breaks outcomes into true/false positives and negatives.

## Why It Matters

- Reveals class-specific failure modes hidden by aggregate accuracy.
- Supports threshold tuning and metric selection for imbalanced data.
