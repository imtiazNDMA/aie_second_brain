# TensorDataset

## Definition

Lightweight PyTorch dataset wrapper that packs tensors into indexable samples for use with [[DataLoader]].

## Why It Matters

- Simplifies supervised dataset creation for experiments.
- Provides a clean bridge from raw tensors to reusable training loops.
