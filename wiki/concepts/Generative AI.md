# Generative AI

## Definition

Models that synthesize new content—text, code, images, audio, or video—by learning probability distributions over massive corpora. Includes VAEs, GANs, autoregressive transformers, and diffusion systems that power agentic behavior.

## Key Points

- Enables agent cognition beyond retrieval by producing plans, code, and natural language responses aligned to prompts.
- Training data breadth introduces safety/ethics obligations; governance chapters in *Building Agentic AI Systems* stress provenance and transparency.
- Generative models often act as the reasoning core while auxiliary components handle tools, memory, and evaluation.
