# Day 0 Architecture

## Definition

Initial architecture for launching a product quickly with clear operational boundaries and room for iterative scale-up.

## Why It Matters

- Prioritizes speed to market and correctness over premature complexity.
- Establishes baseline observability and service seams needed for later growth.
