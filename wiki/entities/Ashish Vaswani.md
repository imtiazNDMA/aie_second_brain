# Ashish Vaswani

Lead author of *Attention Is All You Need* and co-creator of the [[Transformer]] architecture. Worked at Google Brain/Research focusing on sequence modeling and attention mechanisms.

## Contributions

- Introduced scaled dot-product attention and multi-head attention.
- Demonstrated transformer superiority on WMT translation benchmarks.
