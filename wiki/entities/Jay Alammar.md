# Jay Alammar

## Summary
Creator of the famous **Transformer Explainer** visualization and author of **Hands-On Large Language Models** (O'Reilly, 2024). Known for making complex LLM concepts accessible through visual explanations.

## Key Works
- **Hands-On Large Language Models** (with Maarten Grootendorst) — comprehensive guide to LLM architecture, prompting, RAG, fine-tuning
- **Transformer Explainer** — viral visual guide to transformer architecture
- **The Illustrated Transformer** — blog series on NLP architectures
