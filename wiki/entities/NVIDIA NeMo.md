# NVIDIA NeMo

## Summary

NVIDIA’s framework for building, fine-tuning, and serving foundation models with GPU-optimized components (Megatron, NeMo Guardrails, NeMo Retriever). Highlighted in the Ultimate Guide as an industrial platform covering data curation through deployment.

## Capabilities

- Megatron-LM training pipelines with tensor/pipeline parallelism.
- Guardrails for conversation policies, retrieval integration.
- NeMo Inference Microservices (NIMs) for containerized serving.

## Usage

- Train or adapt multi-billion parameter models on DGX clusters.
- Package outputs into NIM containers for Kubernetes-serving.
