# SageMaker JumpStart

## Summary

AWS SageMaker’s catalog of pretrained models, solution templates, and automated deployment pipelines. The Ultimate Guide to Fine-Tuning references JumpStart for provisioning hardware, managing tuning jobs, and publishing endpoints.

## Capabilities

- One-click deployment for popular LLMs with autoscaling.
- Managed training jobs with built-in monitoring/logging.
- Integration with SageMaker pipelines, Clarify, and Model Registry.

## Usage

- Launch PEFT experiments (LoRA/DoRA) without manual cluster setup.
- Expose tuned checkpoints via HTTPS endpoints and integrate with [[LLM Ops Toolchain]].
