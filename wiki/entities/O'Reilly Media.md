# O'Reilly Media

**Entity Type:** Organization  
**Industry:** Technology Publishing, Education  
**Known For:** Technical books, online learning, conferences

## Summary

O'Reilly Media is a prominent American learning company that publishes technology books, offers online learning platforms, and hosts technology conferences. They are well-known in the tech industry for their "In a Nutshell" series and animal-covered books on various programming and technology topics. The company published "LLMOps: Managing Large Language Models in Production" by Abi Aryan in July 2025.
