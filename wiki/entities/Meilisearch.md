# Meilisearch

Open-source search engine offering fast keyword + vector retrieval, frequently cited for standing up RAG prototypes.

## Summary

- Publishes guidance on RAG architectures and tooling (e.g., “14 types of RAG”).
- Supports hybrid retrieval, filters, and embedding ingestion via a simple API.
- Often paired with LangChain, Weaviate, Faiss, or Haystack in modular RAG stacks.

## Connections

- Related to [[Retrieval-Augmented Generation]] implementations where fast hybrid search is required.
