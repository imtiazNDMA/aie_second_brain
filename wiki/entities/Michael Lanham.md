# Micheal Lanham

Author of *AI Agents in Action* and practitioner focused on helping builders ship autonomous assistants that blend LLM reasoning with tool execution.

## Summary

- Frames agents as composites of personas, actions, knowledge, reasoning, and planning.
- Shares hands-on guidance for OpenAI APIs, [[LM Studio]] setups, GPT Assistants, [[AutoGen]], CrewAI, [[Semantic Kernel]], and Nexus.
- Advocates for rigorous evaluation pipelines ([[Prompt Flow]], rubrics) and observable agent runs.
