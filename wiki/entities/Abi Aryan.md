# Abi Aryan

**Entity Type:** Person  
**Profession:** AI/ML Researcher, LLMOps Expert, Author  
**Affiliations:** Abide (Founder), Former Visiting Research Scholar at UCLA Cognitive Systems Lab

## Summary

Abi Aryan is an AI/ML researcher and expert in Large Language Model Operations (LLMOps). She is the founder of Abide and author of "LLMOps: Managing Large Language Models in Production." Her background includes work as a visiting research scholar at the UCLA Cognitive Systems Lab under Dr. Judea Pearl, where she focused on developing intelligent agents and reflective intelligence in AI systems. She brings nearly a decade of experience building production-level ML systems.
